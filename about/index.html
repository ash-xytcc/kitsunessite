About Kitsune

I’m a multidisciplinary artist whose comics turn fleeting feelings, small incidents, private moments, pain, and resolution into something tangible. I make them to express things I cannot always get out through ordinary language, preserving emotions that might otherwise disappear as quickly as they arrived.

My comics are also a way of communicating with my Kitty Bunny. Together, they become a shared visual language made from memory, tenderness, hurt, humor, strange creatures, and the process of finding a way through.

Everything here is independently created, algorithm-free, and available to read for free forever. No feeds deciding what you see, no account required, and no work hidden behind a paywall. Donations are never required, but they help support a struggling artist and make it possible for me to keep creating. Support my work on Venmo.
